Cell-free DNA (cfDNA) arises from the natural breakdown of fetal cells (presumed to be mostly placental). The NIPT analyses cell-free DNA in the maternal blood which can indicate the likelihood of Down's syndrome (Trisomy 21), Edwards Syndrome (Trisomy 18) and Patau's Syndrome (Trisomy 13). On demand we can also yield an outcome for the gender of your child, all from a maternal blood sample.
