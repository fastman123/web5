HEATING AND COOLING air pump will prevent freeze-up

Freeze-up inside your cooling system might take place because of water vapor existing in the system. As the cooling agent undergoes the typical procedure of compression, heating, dissipation and also cooling, the water vapor can start taking shape at the coldest component around the growth unit and also the icy crystals trigger a "freeze-up". In the best situation, this can stop the system from operating. In the most awful situation, this can harm the compressor or the evaporator device. Regardless, to stop freeze-ups, all the moisture has to be gotten rid of from the system before charging it with a brand-new cooling agent. This will certainly be finished with a high quality, high vacuum electric pump.

Prevent rust

Corrosion, besides the freeze-up is another consequence of excessive water vapor residue inside the air conditioning system. The water itself will certainly have corroding result on the within metal walls of the a/c system. Worse, when combined with other highly responsive compounds inside such as chlorine substances and also fluorine compounds, water vapor can trigger incurable corrosion damage. And that kind of damages, given that it is hidden from the eye, will not expose itself up until it is far too late. A regularly employed high vacuum pump will stop rust by eliminating the water vapor from the system.

Avoid mixing of old and also brand-new refrigerant

When updating the refrigerant to a brand-new sort of cooling agent, or when changing the old cooling agent with a fresh refrigerant, making use of a COOLING AND HEATING pump will permit full removal of the old refrigerant. That will certainly permit the system to run to the specs as they relate to the pure brand-new cooling agent. The air conditioning system will have the ability to operate more effectively that way.
