A/C vacuum pump will prevent freeze-up

Freeze-up inside your cooling system might occur due to water vapor existing in the system. As the refrigerant experiences the typical procedure of compression, heating, dissipation as well as air conditioning, the water vapor could begin crystallizing at the chilliest component around the growth unit as well as the icy crystals trigger a "freeze-up". In the very best instance, this can quit the system from operating. In the most awful instance, this can damage the compressor or the evaporator device. Either way, to prevent freeze-ups, all the dampness should be gotten rid of from the system before recharging it with a brand-new cooling agent. This will be performed with a quality, high vacuum cleaner electrical pump.

Stop rust

Rust, besides the freeze-up is one more repercussion of too much water vapor residue inside the air conditioning system. The water itself will certainly have corroding impact on the within metal walls of the cooling system. Worse, when integrated with other very reactive compounds inside such as chlorine substances as well as fluorine substances, water vapor can cause irreparable deterioration damage. And that kind of damages, given that it is concealed from the eye, will not disclose itself up until it is far too late. A frequently utilized high air pump will prevent deterioration by getting rid of the water vapor from the system.

Avoid mixing of old as well as new cooling agent

When upgrading the cooling agent to a brand-new type of cooling agent, or when changing the old refrigerant with a fresh refrigerant, making use of a HEATING AND COOLING pump will enable total elimination of the old cooling agent. That will certainly permit the system to run to the specs as they relate to the pure brand-new refrigerant. The cooling system will certainly be able to run more effectively in this way.
