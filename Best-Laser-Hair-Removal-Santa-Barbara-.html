How to Prepare for Laser Hair Elimination

Laser hair removal is more than just" zapping" unwanted hair. It is a medical treatment that needs training to perform and carries prospective dangers. Prior to getting laser hair elimination, you should thoroughly check the qualifications of the medical professional or technician carrying out the procedure.

If you are planning on undergoing laser hair removal, you must restrict plucking, waxing, and electrolysis for six weeks before treatment. That's because the laser targets the hairs' roots, which are temporarily eliminated by waxing or plucking.

You ought to also avoid sun exposure for 6 weeks before and after treatment. Sun direct exposure makes laser hair elimination less effective and makes problems after treatment more likely.
