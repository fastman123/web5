A medical facial follows the exact same basic steps as a timeless day spa facial, including cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, and hydration. The main distinction between these two treatments is that medical facials are carried out in a physician's office using medical-grade products and tools. The general results are therefore appear more effective.

Given that medical facials are carried out within a physician's office, the skin care expert administering the treatment has the ability to impact the skin at a much deeper level. For example, a low concentration chemical peel might be utilized during a traditional medical spa facial, whereas high-concentration, unbuffered acids can be utilized throughout a medical facial.

Medical facials are extremely customizable and can be customized to your particular skin requirements and issues. Additional treatment add-ons such a dermaplaning might be integrated to physically exfoliate the leading layer of the skin and enhance the absorption of the chemical peel option. Medical facials are likewise a reliable complement to in-office treatments such as dermal fillers and laser skin resurfacing.

Because medical facials are highly customizable, they can successfully attend to a wide spectrum of skin concerns, making them proper for almost everybody. Your skincare professional will work with you to comprehend your particular skin requirements and will advise a personalized medical facial treatment strategy.

Although medical facials can quickly be personalized utilizing a variety of tools and products, there are some conditions that might prevent treatment including use of medications that render skin very delicate. Just like any kind of facial treatment, particular active components and techniques such as laser resurfacing should be prevented if you are pregnant or have a heart disease. It is very important that you go over with your skin care professional any allergies, skin sensitivity, underlying conditions, or medications being taken prior to receiving a medical facial.
