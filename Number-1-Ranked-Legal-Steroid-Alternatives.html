Creatine

Creatine is probably the most popular of all legal steroid supplements known to man. It is a naturally occurring substance in the body and can be found in everyday foods such as meat poultry and fish. It is also an available supplement that can be taken daily to increase your body's creatine levels.

Studies have proven that there are no negative side effects from using creatine over the long term,just be diligent in determining if there are any extra ingredients other than creatine that may have side effects or be a cause for concern.

Matrix Metalloproteinase

MMP is a unique blend of dendrobium, betaine and creatine that is often marketed as CRAZE or by other various names.
This legal supplement is deemed safe to use. However, it doesn't have the muscles-building effects this supplement's advertising claims might have you believe.

Dimethylamylamine (DMAA)

DMAA is an active ingredient in many workout supplements, but it's not entirely safe. Any supplement that contains DMAA and markets itself as a legal supplement is actually illegal.
The FDA warns consumers against taking any dietary supplements that contain Dimethylamylamine.
