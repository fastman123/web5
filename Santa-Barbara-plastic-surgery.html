How do I know if I’m a good candidate for plastic surgery?


An individual who is in good health is a good candidate for plastic surgery. A patient is a also a good candidate for cosmetic surgery if they have realistic goals for the outcome and results of the procedure. It always makes good sense to have in depth discussion with the plastic surgeon during consultation and afterwards to assess candidacy and to make sure the expectations are set correctly. Dr. Sheffield will take the time and answer all questions during the consultation. Additional follow up questions are recommended. Am I too young or too old for plastic surgery?

Generally speaking, there is no steadfast age requirement. Patients in their 20s get Botox and Filler injections to help ease the aging process. Patients who are in their 70s get facelifts. It is a case by case basis. Dr. Sheffield will evaluate all of the factors during the initial consultation such as body type, patient goals, overall health, size and age.
