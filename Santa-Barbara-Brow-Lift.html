How do wrinkles in the brow area form?

Frown lines or wrinkles in the brow area, are caused by facial tension and the repeated use of the active muscles in the brow area. These contract with he muscles in the forehead area which also cause deep lines to form. Tension is what cause these lines and wrinkles to form. Surgery will generally solve the issue, however, Botox might be considered if the wrinkles are not too deep.
